## 📱 3. Meta Ads (Facebook/Instagram)

### Key Trends
- **90% of Meta inventory will be vertical (9:16) in 2026**
- **New "Andromeda" algorithm** changed paid social dynamics
- **Advantage+ campaigns** using ML to find highest-value users
- **Immersive formats**: virtual try-ons, 3D visualization favored
- **Frequency >3.5 impressions = 19% higher cost-per-result**

### Pricing Dynamics
- Meta ads more expensive in 2026 (higher competition)
- CPC varies by seasonality (Q4 = highest costs)
- AI-powered content creation tools now promoted in Instagram "For you" feed

### Strategic Shifts
- Clean data + targeted audiences + authentic creative = performance
- Fresh campaign launches reset algorithm delivery (combats fatigue)
- Shift between interest targeting ↔ Advantage+ based on performance

### Opportunities
- Vertical video creative agencies/tools
- AI ad creative generators specifically for Meta
- CPM optimization consulting (frequency capping)
- Advantage+ Shopping campaign management
