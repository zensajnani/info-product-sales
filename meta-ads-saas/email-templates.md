## Email Deliverability Best Practices

### Technical Setup
- DKIM, SPF, and DMARC records properly configured
- Dedicated sending domain (mail.adflow.ai)
- Dedicated IP address with warming protocol
- SSL/TLS encryption for all emails

### Content Guidelines
- Keep subject lines under 50 characters
- Preview text should be 40-100 characters
- Email width: 600px max
- Dark mode compatible design
- Alt text on all images
- Unsubscribe link in footer (required)

### Sending Schedule
- Welcome: Immediate (triggered)
- Day 1: 24 hours after signup
- Day 3: Conditional (if automation created)
- Day 7: Morning (9am user's timezone)
- Day 11: Morning
- Day 14: Morning
- Monthly: First Monday of each month
- Win-back: 7 days after cancellation

### Segmentation Strategy
1. **By Plan:** Starter/Growth/Pro/Enterprise get different messaging
2. **By Activity:** Active users vs. Setup-incomplete vs. Dormant
3. **By Use Case:** E-commerce, Agencies, SaaS, Lead Gen
4. **By Geography:** Adjust timing and examples

### A/B Testing Priorities
1. Subject lines (highest impact)
2. CTA button text and placement
3. Send time optimization
4. Email length (short vs. detailed)
5. Personalization depth

### Metrics to Track
- Open rate target: >35%
- Click rate target: >8%
- Reply rate target: >2%
- Conversion rate target: >15% (trial to paid)
- Unsubscribe rate: <0.5%
- Spam complaint rate: <0.1%
