- **Conversational** but professional

- **Honest**—no false scarcity or exaggerated claims

1. **Time reclaimed** (15+ hours/week)
2. **Ease of use** (no-code, intuitive)
3. **Power** (AI + real-time + scale)
4. **Trust** (security, support, reliability)
5. **Value** (fair pricing, unlimited users)
